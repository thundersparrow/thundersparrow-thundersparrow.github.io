About us

Thunder Sparrow is a creative studio composed of designers and engineers who combine interactivity and latest technologies to create three dimensional immersive experiences. We create immersive audio-visual applications that catch the attention of audiences and fascinate by their originality.

We offer tailor-made design, from conception to implementation and continuous service. Thunder Sparrow is specialized in the development and commercialization of interactive products and creative services with special focus in virtual, augmented and mixed reality.

Collaborating with companies that are active in digital communication and cultural mediation, we are able to create high quality projects in a variety of fields, such as: retails, museums, exhibitions, trade fairs, events, stores, points of sale, foyers and showrooms.

Services

Virtual Reality

We create virtual reality projects that allow us to live an immersive, visual, sonar and sensory experience. By putting on a virtual reality headset, users are immersed in a virtually generated environment that is fully interactive. This technology has many applications at different levels. It allows you to (re)live an entertaining experience or to help you acquire new skills or strengthen the already existing ones.

Augmented Reality

We specialize in augmented reality that amplifies reality using virtual elements (sounds, 2D objects, 3D objects, videos). This technology inserts virtual content into a sequence of real images taken with a camera, smartphone, tablet or special glasses. It provides additional or complementary information on a given reality, whether it is for entertaining purposes or for display of practical information.

Mixed Reality

We create memorable experiences that combine the virtual environment and the real environment. This combination creates new spaces integrating the best of both worlds. This is currently possible thanks to two types of technology: holograms, three-dimensional images projected onto the real world and HoloLens, which is a pair of augmented reality glasses for simulating holograms in the user's field of vision.

3D

We make your ideas visible. We have the expertise to create objects, animations, places or landscapes in 3D according to your needs. We used and successfully deployed projects using technologies and tools ranging from C++ to real-time web services.

Programming

If the client needs a custom software solution, we can provide it using our technical expertise in the field of software development and engineering.

Physical Computing

We put in place installations with screens, projectors and sensors that are easy to maintain. We also manufacture electronic and hardware solutions that interact with the installations in order to provide the users with a unique and creative experience.
